<h4><a id="user-content-procedures" class="anchor" aria-hidden="true" href="#procedures"><svg class="octicon octicon-link" viewBox="0 0 16 16" version="1.1" width="16" height="16" aria-hidden="true"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a>Procedures</h4>

<p>The first game we played was the classic "name game". Kids were very excited and eager to meet the challenge. The rules were:</p>

<p>We played a game whose purpose was to make sure everyone knew some basic vocabulary for user actions, and how to perform the actions. Since I expected to have to use this vocabulary often, I wanted to be sure the kids would know it. The rules were:</p>
<ul>
<li>If I say "Teacher Says" it means everyone does what I say.</li>
<li>If I don't say "Teacher Says", no one does anything.</li>
<li>If I don't say it, and you do it, you have to kneel for one turn.</li>
<li>If I  say it, and you don't do it, you have to kneel for one turn.</li>
</ul>
<p>The kinds of vocabulary and actions we practiced were:</p>
<ul>
<li>

<ul>
<li><strong>Tap</strong> your <em>nose</em>
<ul>

<li><strong>Tap</strong>  <em>slowly, quickly, smoothly, roughly, soft, hard</em></li>
<li><strong>Double Tap</strong> your nose</li>
<li>same with <em>ear</em>, <em>knee</em>, <em>table</em>, som <em>classmate</em>, <em>computer</em></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Tap and Hold</strong></li>
<li><strong>Double Tap and Hold</strong></li>
<li><strong>Let go</strong></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Second Set: Drag and Scroll</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Tap and Drag on</strong> on the table.
<ul>
<li><strong>this means TAP and HOLD and DRAG and (sometimes) LET GO</strong></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li><strong>scroll up</strong>, <strong>scroll down</strong>, <strong>scroll right</strong>, <strong>scroll left</strong></li>
